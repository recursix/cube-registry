WorkArena

workarena · v1.0.0

📄 Paper
web gui

WorkArena evaluates agents on enterprise service-desk workflows inside a real ServiceNow Personal Developer Instance. Tasks are organized into three levels: L1 atomic tasks (~33 unique tasks × multiple seeds), L2 compositional tasks, and L3 extended tasks with company protocols. Requires a free ServiceNow PDI and browser-automation tooling.

By: @younik (Omar Younis) , @NicolasAG (Nicolas Gontier)
